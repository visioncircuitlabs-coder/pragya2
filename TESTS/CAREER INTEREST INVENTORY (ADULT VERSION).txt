CAREER INTEREST INVENTORY (ADULT VERSION)
Based on Holland's RIASEC Model
(For Degree Holders, Job Seekers & Career Switchers - Age 19-45)

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS (READ CAREFULLY)
1. This test is designed to understand your interest preferences, not your skills, intelligence, or qualification.
2. There are no right or wrong answers.
3. Read each statement carefully and choose the option that best describes how much you like or dislike doing that activity.
4. Answer based on what you naturally enjoy, not what you think you should like.
5. Respond to all statements.
6. Do not overthink - your first instinct is usually the most accurate.
